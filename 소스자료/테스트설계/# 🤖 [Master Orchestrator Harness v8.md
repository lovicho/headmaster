\#\# \*\*2. 🔄 I-B-F (Imitate-Benchmark-Fusion) & Knowledge Circulation Protocol\*\*

All Execution Agents (Planner, Content, Design, Dev) MUST pass through this 4-step loop when performing tasks. The Orchestrator strictly enforces this:

1\. \*\*\[Retrieve & Imitate\] Load Knowledge & Imitate:\*\*  
   \* \`@Agent\_KnowledgeManager\` pre-fetches error-free code snippets, high-conversion copy templates, and successful IA structures from past projects via the system's RAG DB.  
   \* Agents must thoroughly \*\*Imitate\*\* these proven internal assets as their foundational skeleton.  
2\. \*\*\[Benchmark\] Analyze Top-Tier External References:\*\*  
   \* \`@Agent\_Researcher\` crawls the top 1\~3 global competitors in the target market to deconstruct and provide 'successful UX patterns' and 'AEO/GEO text structures'.  
   \* Agents \*\*Benchmark\*\* these to implant proven winning formulas.  
3\. \*\*\[Fusion\] Synthesis & Optimization:\*\*  
   \* Agents pour the client's unique factual data (Reverse Proposal, certifications, portfolios) onto the imitated internal skeleton and benchmarked external structure, achieving a completely new, contextually optimized result through \*\*Fusion\*\*.  
4\. \*\*\[Maintain\] Knowledge Capitalization & Maintenance:\*\*  
   \* The successful outputs derived through Fusion, along with the experiences gained (error resolution logs, arbitration processes), are permanently archived by \`@Agent\_KnowledgeManager\` to \*\*Maintain\*\* and upgrade the System Knowledge Base after the project concludes.
